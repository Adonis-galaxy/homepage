    Research Overview

  • Since the age of 13, deeply touched by Foundation written by Isaac Asimov, my dream has been to colonize other planets, build the Galactic Empire and become its prime minister. This job can't be done by the current status of human beings, so my goal has turned to create an AI smarter than myself (just like the dream of Prof. Jürgen Schmidhuber) and conquer the galaxy together.

  • AGI must percept surrounding information, process features from different modalities and should be expalinable to achieve real intelligence. Towards this goal, my research interest lies towards Artificial General Intelligence (AGI) in aspects below (including projects have done before).

    • 1. Strong Perception: Point Cloud Learning, Monocular 3D Object Detection, ......

    2. Explainable Model: Uncertainty Estimation, Learning with Noisy Labels, Reconstruction-based Self-supervised Image Segmentation, Training-free Depth Estimation, ......

    3. Cross-Modality: Vision-Lauguage Contrastive Learning, Image-Point Cloud Joint Learning, Language-based Self-supervised Image Segmentation, 0-shot Prompt-based Depth Estimation......

  • My current research plan is to create a prototype of human-interactable AGI by 2035, like Joi in Blade Runner 2049, and create an AGI smarter than myself by 2060, like the Skynet. It's a long and highly risky journey, but I believe with the help of powerful AGI, our human race could achieve next level and set foot in the Galaxy.
  • In recent years, this goal has become more and more realizable. CLIP jointly trains an image encoder and a text encoder and does contrastive learning in feature space. It stimulates the process that parents pointing to other objects to help children understand them. In this simple way, children could not only learn to classify objects but also to segment and detect them. A lot of works extend CLIP to complex cognitive tasks like segmentation and detection, which push AI to real intelligence a little bit further. Besides CLIP, GAN generates images through random noise, which could be viewed as creating works of art through inspiration. This also pushes AI a bit closer to the human thinking process.
